BASIC OF ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING

Let A = area of X-section of the wire
n = electron density i.e number of free electrons per unit volume
e = charge on each electron
vd = drift velocity of free electrons
I = n e A vd
Since A, n and e are constant

Hence, current flowing through a conductor is directly proportional to the drift velocity of free electrons.
